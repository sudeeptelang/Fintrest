Frequently asked questions
Is this financial advice? ▼
No. Fintrest.ai provides educational research signals and data-driven analytics only. Nothing on this platform constitutes financial advice, investment recommendations, or a solicitation to buy or sell securities. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
How does the AI scoring work? ▼
Our deterministic rule engine scores each stock across 7 weighted dimensions: momentum (25%), relative volume (15%), news catalyst (15%), earnings/fundamentals (15%), sentiment (10%), trend strength (10%), and risk filter (10%). AI is used only to translate these structured scores into human-readable explanations — it does not originate the signal itself.
When are signals delivered? ▼
Pro and Premium members receive early morning alerts at 6:30 AM EST with the top picks from the overnight scan. Additional intraday signals are sent when strong setups emerge during market hours. Starter members receive delayed alerts at 9:00 AM.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked.
What markets do you cover? ▼
Currently we cover US-listed equities across NYSE, NASDAQ, and AMEX — approximately 2,800+ tickers daily. India market coverage (NSE/BSE) is coming in Phase 3 of our roadmap, available to Premium subscribers first.
